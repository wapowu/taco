Chapultepec Park (Bosque de Chapultepec)

Mexico City's massive green heart—four times the size of Central Park—Chapultepec is packed with nature, history, and world-class museums. The park is divided into multiple sections and has something for everyone: royal history, paddle boats, food stalls, trails, and lush gardens.

Top attractions inside the park include:

  • 🏰 Chapultepec Castle – hilltop castle with sweeping views and rich Mexican history
  • 🏛️ National Museum of Anthropology – home of the Aztec Sun Stone and ancient Mesoamerican treasures
  • 🖼️ Modern Art Museum – featuring works by Rivera, Kahlo, Tamayo, and more
  • 🦁 Chapultepec Zoo – free and family-friendly with over 200 species
  • 🚣‍♂️ Paddle Boats – rent a boat and relax on the lakes
  • 🌮 Street Food Vendors – grab elotes, ice cream, snacks while you explore

Good to know: Most areas do not require reservations, but popular spots like the castle or anthropology museum are best visited early or with advance tickets to avoid lines.
